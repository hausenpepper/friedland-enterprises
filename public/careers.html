Executive Assistant
/ Chief of Staff

Miami Beach, FL Part-Time to Full-Time 1099 Contractor Remote-Friendly

What This Actually Is

This is not a conventional EA role, and if that's what you're looking for, this isn't it.

The principal runs a profitable e-commerce startup, holds several board seats across the U.S. and internationally, and manages an active investment portfolio, all while being a present parent and homeowner mid-renovation. The connective tissue holding all of that together is currently held by AI systems, a shared family office, and willpower. You are the human layer that makes all of it actually work.

Most of this role can be done remotely. Some of it — managing contractors, overseeing household operations, handling physical logistics — requires being nearby in Miami Beach. If you only want one or the other, this is not the right fit.

What makes this different is the AI component. The principal runs heavily automated systems for email triage, research, board preparation, financial reconciliation, and more. Your job is not to do those things manually. It is to operate those systems, keep them running, improve them over time, and step in with judgment when automation hits its limits. The person who thrives here sees that as exciting, not threatening.

You will also be embedded in real business decisions, board-level dynamics, investment research, and international operations including active relationships in Brazil. If you are ambitious and intellectually curious, the exposure here is hard to replicate anywhere else.

This starts part-time with a clear path to full-time for the right person.

What You'll Own

On the household side, you will manage an active home renovation project in Miami Beach, coordinating contractors, tracking timelines, attending walkthroughs, and catching problems before they escalate. You will own recurring home services including cleaning, pressure washing, landscaping, pool, and HVAC — managing vendor relationships and holding people accountable to a standard the principal does not have to think about. Day-to-day logistics like grocery runs, pharmacy, dry cleaning, and errands are part of it. Property matters beyond Miami Beach are handled through a shared family office, and you will coordinate with that team rather than manage them directly.

On the executive and business side, the principal uses AI to handle the bulk of email triage, board preparation, and research synthesis. Your job is to manage and improve those systems, flag what the AI misses, prepare briefings, and handle execution on the output — calendar management, follow-ups, vendor communications, and anything that requires a human touch or local presence.

On the financial side, reconciliation is handled by AI. Your job is anomaly flagging, identifying charges that don't belong, and keeping the principal's personal and business finances organized at a summary level. This is not a bookkeeping role — it is an oversight role.

What You Need

You need to be located within 20–30 minutes of Miami Beach. Most work is remote, but physical availability several times per week is required — especially during the renovation phase.

Experience managing a home renovation or construction project is a strong plus. A background supporting a high-output executive, founder, or principal in a chief of staff or EA capacity is relevant. Familiarity with AI tools — even at a basic level — is required. You don't need to be a developer, but you need to be genuinely comfortable working inside automated systems and learning new tools quickly.

This is not the right fit for someone who needs structure handed to them, wants predictable uniform days, or is looking for a traditional support role. The right person creates structure, thrives in ambiguity, and has a bias toward figuring things out rather than waiting to be told.

Who We're Looking For

There is no single archetype here, but a few profiles come to mind.

Someone who spent years in operations, project management, or a chief of staff role, stepped back to take time off or raise a family, and is now ready to re-engage — part-time first, with room to grow.

Someone early in their career who is extraordinarily organized, self-directed, and hungry to learn by proximity to real business decisions rather than through a traditional career track.

Someone who has run their own household, business, or team at a high level and understands intuitively what it means to hold systems together while the principal focuses forward.

The common thread across all of them: a genuine desire to serve and to automate. Not to be served.

The Opportunity

The right person grows into a true partner here, not an assistant. You will be close to real decisions — board dynamics, investment underwriting, operational problem-solving across multiple businesses — and you will have a front-row seat to how an operator-investor runs a complex, multi-entity principal life at scale.

This starts at 20–25 hours a week. The path to full-time is performance, not time served.

Compensation

Pay is commensurate with experience. This is a 1099 independent contractor engagement to start.
